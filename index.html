Legal & Registration Services

Land Registration

End-to-end support for property and land registration. Document preparation, guideline value calculation, and SRO submission.

Marriage Registration

Hassle-free marriage registration under Special Marriage Act & Hindu Marriage Act. Application filing and certificate procurement.

Rental Agreement

Professionally drafted rental/lease agreements. Typing, printing, and stamp paper — everything handled at our office.

Sale Deed Drafting

Expert drafting of sale deeds with all essential clauses. Customized to your property transaction requirements.

EC (Encumbrance Certificate)

Procurement of EC from sub-registrar office to verify property ownership history and check for any legal liabilities.

Gift Deed & Settlement Deed

Professional preparation of gift deeds and settlement deeds for smooth property transfer within family members.

Certified Copy

Assistance in obtaining certified copies of registered documents, sale deeds, and ECs from the Sub-Registrar Office.

Will Drafting

Professional drafting and registration of Will documents to ensure your assets are distributed according to your wishes.

Unregistered Sale Deed

Expert typing and drafting of unregistered sale deeds for specific transactions and mutual agreements.

Stamp Paper, Affidavit & Notary

Stamp Paper (e-Stamp)

e-Stamp papers of all denominations available instantly. Used for agreements, affidavits, bonds, and legal documents.

Affidavit Preparation

Typing and preparation of affidavits for name change, address proof, income, gas transfer, and all legal purposes.

Notary Services

Notary attestation for affidavits, agreements, and other legal documents. Quick attestation with authorized notary public.

Legal Notice Typing

Professional typing of legal notices, petition drafts, and court document preparation with accuracy and confidentiality.

Power of Attorney

Drafting of General and Special Power of Attorney documents. Complete guidance on type selection and registration process.

Xerox, Printing & DTP

Xerox / Photocopying

High-quality black & white and colour xerox copies. Bulk copying at discounted rates for offices and students.

Computer Printing

Laser and inkjet printouts for documents, photos, certificates, and project reports. Single & double-side printing.

Scanning & Email

Document and photo scanning to PDF/JPEG. Direct scan-to-email services for instant digital sharing.
